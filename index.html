Our Story

A Legacy of Authentic Puerto Rican Seafood Excellence

The Birthplace of Mojo Isle√±o

Since 1949, Ladi's Place has been serving the freshest seafood in Puerto Rico. Our historic restaurant is recognized as the creator of the famous Mojo Isle√±o, a culinary tradition that has delighted generations of locals and visitors alike.

What started as a small seaside eatery has grown into a beloved institution with two locations, but our commitment remains the same: serving only the freshest catch, delivered daily by local fishermen who go out each morning specifically to supply our restaurants.

Experience authentic Puerto Rican cuisine in a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere where every meal comes with breathtaking ocean views and warm hospitality.

1949

Ladi's Place opens its doors in Salinas, introducing Puerto Rico to the now-famous Mojo Isle√±o and establishing a tradition of fresh, authentic seafood cuisine.
